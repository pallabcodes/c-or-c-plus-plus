### Certification Programs

#### AuroraDB Certified Administrator (ACA)
- **Prerequisites**: DBA-100 training
- **Exam**: 100 questions, 2 hours
- **Validity**: 2 years
- **Topics**: Installation, configuration, security, backup/recovery, monitoring

#### AuroraDB Certified Developer (ACD)
- **Prerequisites**: DEV-100 training
- **Exam**: 80 questions, 2 hours
- **Validity**: 2 years
- **Topics**: SQL development, query optimization, application integration, testing

#### AuroraDB Certified Security Professional (ACSP)
- **Prerequisites**: SEC-100 training + ACA/ACD
- **Exam**: 120 questions, 3 hours
- **Validity**: 2 years
- **Topics**: Security architecture, compliance, encryption, audit, incident response

#### AuroraDB Certified High Availability Specialist (ACHAS)
- **Prerequisites**: HA-100 training + ACA
- **Exam**: 100 questions, 2.5 hours
- **Validity**: 2 years
- **Topics**: Clustering, replication, failover, load balancing, disaster recovery
