Backtracking works well with:

1. Arrays
   - Example: Subset Sum Problem.

2. Strings
   - Example: Permutations and Combinations of Characters.

3. Grids
   - Example: N-Queens Problem, Sudoku Solver.

4. Graphs
   - Example: Hamiltonian Path, Graph Coloring.

5. Trees
   - Example: All Root-to-Leaf Paths in a Binary Tree.
